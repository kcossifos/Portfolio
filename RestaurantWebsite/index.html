Welcome!

Bonafide is a full-service vegan restaurant located just north of downtown Orlando in Winter Park. Our daily menu is 100% vegan and features hearty, homestyle fare. On Saturday and Sunday we also offer a Brunch menu.

We invite you to browse our website to learn more about us, scan through our recent news or read critics' and diners' reviews. You can also sign up for our monthly newsletter to have Ethos news and announcements delivered direct to your inbox!

Please contact us if you have any questions we have not addressed in the Frequently Asked Questions section, or if you have any comments you'd like to share.

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to meeting you at the restaurant!
