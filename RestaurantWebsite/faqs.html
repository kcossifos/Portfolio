FAQs

What is vegan food? A vegan diet excludes all animal products, comprising vegetables, vegetable oils, grains, legumes, nuts, fruits and seeds. Meat, eggs, dairy products, and other animal derivatives are not consumed.

Do you accept take-out orders? Yes, we do! You can place a take-out order by calling the restaurant at 407-228-3898. Our friendly staff will be happy to let you know about today's specials.

Do you serve alcohol? Yes, we offer a small selection of beer and wine. Did you know that not all alcoholic beverages are vegan? Well, don't worry, we've verified that each of our selections are vegan - free of isinglass, chitosan, albumen, and other animal by-products. Please note: in accordance with local ordinance, BYOB is not acceptable at our restaurant.

Do you accept reservations? No, we are a first-come, first-serve establishment, and reservations are not accepted. Exception: We accept courtesy reservations for parties consisting of eight guests or more. While reservations are not required, we recommend that parties of this size make a reservation during peak meal times: Friday and Saturday dinner, and Sunday Brunch. Please call us at 407-228-3898 to request a reservation.

Do you deliver? Currently we do not offer delivery service. However, we may introduce delivery service to a limited area at some point in the future.

Do you have nutritional information or calorie counts for your menu? We do not have formal nutritional workups for our menu items. Because we make nearly everything from scratch, it is not as simple as reading the label. And, unfortunately, hiring a nutritionist to conduct the analyses has so far been out of reach of our budget. For a rough idea, we suggest looking up a similar food item on a nutritional value website such as CalorieCount.com - the figures won't be exact, but the ballpark should be about the same.
