Location

Bonafide Restaurant, 601-B S New York Ave, Winter Park, FL, 32789. Phone: 407-228-3898

We are located just north of downtown Orlando in Winter Park at the corner of Fairbanks Avenue and New York Avenue, one block west of Park Avenue. See a map of our location below.

Free parking is available in our private parking lot, with an additional overflow lot located across New York Avenue behind Florida Capital Bank. More information on overflow parking is below. Our indoor dining room seats approximately 110 guests. Free WiFi is available. Our entire facility is wheelchair accessible.

Parking

Free parking for our guests is available in either the large private parking lot outside of our front door (accessible from Fairbanks Avenue) or the smaller lot off of New York Avenue. Both lots are reserved for Winter Park Plaza use only, which includes Bonafide!

Tip: If you will be headed west on Fairbanks after leaving our restaurant, you may find it easiest to park in the New York lot and then make your left onto Fairbanks at the traffic light at Fairbanks and New York.

While parking is plentiful, we do share the lot with the other tenants of Winter Park Plaza. On occasion our lots will fill up due to high demand or nearby events. In addition to street parking on side streets, the City of Winter Park provides a number of public parking lots nearby.

If you need help finding an alternate parking spot while behind the wheel, please call us at 407-228-3898 for assistance - we are happy to help!
